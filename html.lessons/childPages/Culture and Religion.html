My religion

What is my religion?

I was born in Romania where a big part of it's population is Christian, so i am a Christian. We believe in God and Jesus Christ.

Our most important celebrations are Christmas and Easter. These days represent the born of Jesus Christ (Christmas) and the death of Him (Easter).

Other celebrations

Other celebrations are saints celebrations, like Saint Constantin and Elena or Saint Maria, Jesus's mother. The people that are named like the saints are receiving a "Happy name-day" from everyone.

During the Christmas Eve, family and friends come together,they bring presents and have a lot of fun talking and cheering Jesus's birthday. We always have a Christmas tree that is full of globes, lights and all those decorations. Easter's Eve is the day that the whole family comes together on the day that Jesus was sacrified on a crucifix. We have chocolate egg hunt and lots of good food.
